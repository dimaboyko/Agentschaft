Team of AI Agents

We believe that the ability to manage Teams of AI Agents (not single chats) will be key for companies’ success. Everyone should be able to become a manager of or a colleague to AI Agents. And we want to make this a reality.

How it works ?

We want to enable everyone with the ability to build teams of AI Agents to support business processes of any complexity. For this, we set our goal to build a conceptual framework with respective tooling, which answers a set of key questions for AI Agents’ team management, like how to optimally convert a business process into a workflow supported by a team of AI Agents? or "How to measure performance of single AI Agent or teams of AI Agents solving (complex) business tasks?"
